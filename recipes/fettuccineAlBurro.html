Fettucine Al Burro

Description

"Pasta Al Burro" is the Italian version of the American pasta dish "Fettuccine Alfredo". In the Italian version it is alot simpler and relies much more heavily on technique. I have made it a couple of times and have loved it each time.

Ingredients

  • One Pound of Fettuccine Pasta either in a Box or Hand Made
  • 2 Cups of Grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Half of Cup of Softened, Unsalted Butter
  • Good Amount of Salt to Season the Water

Instructions

  1. Grab a pot, fill it with water, and then set it to boil
  2. Once your water is boiling salt it until it is "salty like the sea" (very salty)
  3. Place your pasta in the water and cook it specified on the box or until al dente
  4. Place your softened butter in a bowl where we will make the pasta
  5. Once the pasta is al dente save 2 cups of the pasta water for later
  6. Drain the rest of the water and now place your fettuccine in the bowl with the butter
  7. Mix the butter and the fettuccine until all the butter is melted, and once the butter is melted pour your 2 cups of parmigiano reggiano in the bowl
  8. Once again mix until there is a sauce coating on the noodles
  9. Add pasta water while mixing until you get the consistency of the sauce that you like. (Do Not Add too Much Pasta Water at Once)
