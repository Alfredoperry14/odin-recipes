Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a very basic recipe which is very customizable and is a classic that everyone loves.

Ingredients

  • Any type of nut butter can work if you'd like (almond butter, peanut butter, etc).
  • Any type of bread that you like (white bread, wheat, sourdough, etc).
  • Any type of jelly (strawberry, grape, peach, etc).

Instructions

  1. Grab your bread and put it on a plate. Optional: Toast the bread beforehand.
  2. Grab your jelly and spread it across on of the slices of your bread.
  3. Grab your peanut butter and spread it across one of the slices of your peanut butter.
  4. Put your two slices of bread together and then enjoy!
